Waiting for the Bus

After 2nd Period, Chance and I walked to Ms. Sinclair’s to leave our backpacks there until we were back from the trip. Then we walked back to the main lobby to wait for our bus to get here. We formed our little groups and talked until our bus got here. Ms. Sinclair told us to wait so that she could check roll while we got on. Takori, Noah, Caleb, Chance, Fuya, and me went to the back.

Bus Ride to Archer Malmo

We talked and listened to our music for life half the way there. Then someone said we should for a group chat so that we could play some uno. Then we got bored of uno so we all played some 8-ball and cup pong. When we arrived downtown and passed the Bass Pro some of us took pictures of it to put it on our snapchat stories. We got to our stop and we all got off and started walking towards Archer Malmo. We walked inside to the lobby of the building and waited for our elevator to get down.

Archer Malmo

We got on the elevator in like groups of 6 up to like the 4th floor. When I got up there people were playing Ping Pong. We were loud so some guy walked out and said they were in a meeting and told us to be quieter. Then Ms. Sinclair and the speaker got up and he started talking and showing some presentation. I tried to get a chair but I was too late. I had to stand against some pillar. He talked about some of their past projects and showed us a couple. I looked around and about everyone was asleep. About halfway through my legs started to get tired. Luckily I got through it and Ms. Sinclair told us to take pictures for our project. We took the stairs up to their main floor to look around, then they took us to their recording room, where they had green screens and all sorts of stuff. We took the stairs up to the main lobby again and waited for everyone else to get there. Then we walked back down the street to get on the bus. It wasn’t there so we had to wait for it again.

Cook-Out
